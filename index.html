❓ Common Questions — Tap to Read
If you start at 7:00 AM and work 8 hours, you clock out at 3:00 PM. Tap My Shift at the top for any start time.
Tap My Shift → "How Many Hours Did I Work?" Enter your start and end time. It shows hours, minutes, and decimal hours for payroll.
Most states require one 30-minute meal break and two 15-minute rest breaks in an 8-hour shift. Rules vary by state.
Most jobs pay 1.5× your rate for every hour over 40 in a week. Tap My Pay → Overtime to calculate with any rate.
Tap My Pay. Enter your hourly rate, hours, and tax rate (most workers use 20–25%). See gross, taxes out, and real take-home.
