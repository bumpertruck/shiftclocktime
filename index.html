What this site is and why it exists.

We built this for shift workers.

Most people who work shifts spend time every day trying to figure out basic things — what time do I get off? How much did I make today? What does a raise actually mean for my yearly income?

We built Shift Clock Time to answer those questions fast, for free, with no signup and no nonsense. Every tool on this site does one thing well and gets out of your way.

This site is built for nurses, warehouse workers, drivers, factory workers, security guards, restaurant staff, and anyone else who works by the hour and deserves clear answers.

What you can do here

  • Find out what time your shift ends
  • Calculate how many hours you worked
  • Track live earnings as your shift runs
  • See your take-home pay after taxes
  • Calculate overtime pay
  • Fill in and print a weekly timesheet
  • See what a raise would mean for your income

Everything is free. No account needed. No data sold. Just tools that work.

About Shift Clock Time

Why this site exists and what it does

Shift Clock Time is a free tool built for people who work by the hour. Nurses, warehouse workers, drivers, security guards, factory workers, retail staff — anyone who clocks in and clocks out.

Most pay calculators are built for office jobs with set salaries. Shift workers have different needs. You want to know what time you get off. How many hours you worked. What you actually take home after taxes. Whether your overtime was calculated right.

This site answers those questions — fast, for free, with no account required. Every tool works in seconds. Nothing is saved to any server. Your information stays on your device.
